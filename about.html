Our Story

Preserving cartographic treasures and the stories they tell — one map at a time.

Who We Are

Antique Map is a small collective of historians, conservators, and map enthusiasts dedicated to sourcing, restoring, and sharing historical maps from around the world. Founded by collectors who wanted to make rare maps accessible, we combine scholarly research with hands-on conservation.

What We Do

We acquire significant and unusual maps, document their provenance, and restore them when necessary. Our collection focuses on maps that reveal cultural, scientific, and navigational history — from early explorers' charts to hand-drawn city plans.

The Authentication Process

Every piece in our collection undergoes a rigorous vetting process before being offered to the public. We believe that true historical value is rooted in undeniable authenticity.

  • Paper & Ink Analysis: We meticulously examine the specific chain lines, watermarks, and wood-pulp composition to date the paper. Ink oxidation patterns must align perfectly with the purported era.
  • Cartographic Verification: Our historians cross-reference geographical anomalies against known historical records to rule out later reproductions or forgeries.
  • Provenance Tracking: We trace the ownership history of each map through auction records, estate catalogs, and private collections to establish a clear chain of custody.

Our Global Network

Over the past three decades, we have established unparalleled relationships with private collectors, aristocratic estates, and auction houses across Europe and the Americas. This ensures our clients have first-access to maps that rarely see the public market.
