Frequently Asked Questions

Are the physical maps authentic?

Yes, all physical artifacts sold through our gallery undergo rigorous provenance and material analysis by our expert conservators to guarantee absolute authenticity.

How are digital downloads delivered?

Upon subscription, high-resolution scans (up to 8K depending on your tier) are immediately available for download directly from the map detail pages.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes, subscriptions operate on a month-to-month basis. You can upgrade, downgrade, or pause your membership through your account dashboard at any time.

Do you buy maps from private collections?

We are always interested in acquiring rare artifacts. Please contact our acquisitions team via the Contact page to schedule an initial appraisal.
