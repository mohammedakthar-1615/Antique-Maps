Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer fully insured, white-glove shipping to almost every country worldwide. All items are meticulously packed to museum standards in custom archival materials.

Do your maps come with a certificate of authenticity?

Absolutely. Every map we sell is accompanied by a hand-signed, universally recognized Certificate of Authenticity guaranteeing its age, condition, and origin.

Can I schedule a private viewing?

Yes, private viewings are available at our New York and London locations by appointment only. Please select "Schedule a Viewing" in the contact form, specifying the items of interest.

Do you buy maps from private collections?

Yes, our acquisitions team is always looking consistently for high-quality, authentic maps and atlases. Contact us with detailed descriptions and high-resolution photos of your items for a preliminary evaluation.
